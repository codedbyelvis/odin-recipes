Cuban Sandwich

Description

There are many recipes for Cuban sandwiches. Here's how to build a Cuban with pulled pork.

Ingredients

  • 1 (8 ounce) loaf Cuban bread
  • 1 ½ cups cooked pulled pork, heated
  • 6 thin slices smoked fully-cooked ham
  • 8 slices Swiss cheese
  • 1 large dill pickle, sliced thinly lengthwise
  • ¼ cup mayonnaise
  • ¼ cup mustard
  • 1 pinch cayenne pepper
  • 2 tablespoons butter, or as needed

Steps

  1. Mix mayonnaise, mustard, and cayenne together in a bowl to make sauce.
  2. Trim off ends of bread. Cut loaf in half and evenly split bread to make tops and bottoms of two sandwiches.
  3. Spread evenly and generously on all slices of bread with previously-made sauce.
  4. Divide sandwich ingredients between the two bottom halves in this order:
    2 slices of Swiss cheese, 3 slices ham, hot cooked pork, pickle slices, and 2 more slices of Swiss cheese. Place tops on sandwiches.
  5. Melt butter in a heavy skillet over medium heat. Place the sandwiches in the skillet and press them down with a heavy weight, such as another skillet or foil-wrapped bricks. Toast the sandwiches until bread is crisp and filling is heated through, 3 or 4 minutes per side.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy!
