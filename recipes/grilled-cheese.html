Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Description

Learn how to make a grilled cheese sandwich in a nonstick pan with buttered bread and American Cheddar for a classic hot sandwich.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of your bread of choice
  • (Recomendation: Brioche Bread)
  • 1 or 2 slices of your cheese of choice
  • (Recomendation: 2 slices of American Cheddar)
  • 2 tablespoons of your butter of choice
  • (Recomendation: 2 tablespoons of unsalted butter)

Steps

  1. Assemble however many slices of cheese between your bread slices.

    2. (Quick Tip: Have your pan heating up as you assemble your sandwiches.)

  2. Add the 1st tablespoon of butter to the hot pan. Move the pan in a circular motion to make sure the butter spreads evenly.
  3. Gently place your assembled sandwich on the liquid butter. Check every 2 minutes to see if the bottom bread slice has browned.
  4. As the bottom slice finishes browning, add your 2nd tablespoon of butter to the pan so it can melt next to your sandwich.
  5. Once the 2nd tablespoon of butter has become liquid, gently flip your sandwich so the top slice will now cook. Check every 2 minutes to see if the top bread slice has browned.
  6. Once the top slice has browned, remove for the pan and let cool for 1 minute. Repeat these steps for as many sandwiches you want enjoy.
